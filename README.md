## 📽️ The Future of Language Learning & Mastery
**AnkiVoice** is a groundbreaking, open-source project dedicated to the **Anki ecosystem**. It transforms your flashcards from static text into an immersive, voice-driven dialogue. Designed for polyglots, medical students, and power learners, AnkiVoice lets you master your decks while your eyes are off the screen.

### 💎 Elite Features:
- **Neural Voice Synthesis**: Powered by **Piper**, providing human-like feedback and question delivery.
- **Whisper AI Integration**: State-of-the-art **Speech-to-Text** that understands your answers even in noisy environments.
- **Hands-Free Active Recall**: Review your cards while commuting, exercising, or relaxing. Just speak to your agent.
- **Deep AnkiDroid Integration**: Direct secure connection to your Anki database. Your progress is synced instantly.

---

## 🚀 Get Started in Seconds

### 1. Zero-Config Installation
Grab the latest APK from our [Releases](https://github.com/safevoice009/podcards-ai-engine/releases) page. Ensure you have **AnkiDroid** installed for database access.

### 2. Premium AI Engine Setup
For maximum performance, AnkiVoice runs 100% locally. Download the following optimized models:
1.  **Whisper (STT)**: [ggml-tiny.en.bin](https://huggingface.co/ggerganov/whisper.cpp/resolve/main/ggml-tiny.en.bin)
2.  **Piper (TTS)**: [en_US-lessac-low.onnx](https://huggingface.co/rhasspy/piper-voices/resolve/v1.0.0/en/en_US/lessac/low/en_US-lessac-low.onnx)
3.  **Directory**: Move them to `/Android/data/com.ankivoice.agent/files/models/`
